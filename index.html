Prayer times for iPhone

VaktPray

An offline iOS app focused exclusively on Islamic prayer times for 🇧🇦 Bosnia and Herzegovina, with daily prayer notifications, widgets, night-time information, and Apple Watch support. No data collected. The full app UI, in-app screens, widgets, and Apple Watch views are available equally in English and Bosnian.

App Details

Built for daily prayer time checks

Offline prayer data

Prayer time data is included in the app, so no internet connection is required after installation.

Local reminders

Prayer reminders are scheduled on your device through iOS notification settings.

Widgets

Widgets use the app's private shared storage to display your selected city and prayer schedule.

English and Bosnian

Built for English and Bosnian speaking users, with both languages available throughout the app.

Apple Watch

Apple Watch support keeps prayer time information available from your wrist.
